A Legacy of Artistic Excellence

Welcome to BYCPAC. For about two decades, we have been dedicated to the promotion and preservation of traditional Chinese performing arts. Our academy offers a comprehensive curriculum designed for students of all ages and skill levels.

Through rigorous training in classical dance, folk styles, and traditional instruments, we aim to nurture not just performers, but cultural ambassadors who carry our heritage forward into the modern world.
