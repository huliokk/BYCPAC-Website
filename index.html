A Legacy of Artistic Excellence 艺术卓越的传承

Welcome to BYCPAC. For about two decades, we have been dedicated to the promotion and preservation of traditional Chinese performing arts. Our academy offers a comprehensive curriculum designed for students of all ages and skill levels. 欢迎来到BYCPAC。近二十年来，我们致力于推广和传承中国传统表演艺术。我们的学院提供适合各年龄段和水平学生的全面课程体系。

Through rigorous training in classical dance, folk styles, and traditional instruments, we aim to nurture not just performers, but cultural ambassadors who carry our heritage forward into the modern world. 通过严格的古典舞蹈、民族风格及传统乐器训练，我们不仅培养表演者，更培育将中华文化传承至现代世界的文化使者。

Master the intricate movements, leaps, and tumbling techniques that define this ancient art form. 掌握古典舞的精妙动作、跳跃与翻腾技巧，感受这门古老艺术的魅力。

Peking Opera京剧

Experience China's most celebrated theatrical art — a dazzling blend of song, speech, mime, acrobatics, and elaborate costume that has captivated audiences for over 200 years. 体验中国最负盛名的舞台艺术——京剧集唱腔、念白、哑剧、杂技与华美服饰于一身，两百余年来令无数观众叹为观止。
